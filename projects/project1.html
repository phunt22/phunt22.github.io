REV Mobile

React Native TypeScript
App Store GitHub

Overview

REV Mobile is a delivery app for a high-growth delivery startup serving college campuses. The app has acquired over 6,000 users and generated $600K in sales since launch.

Check out this demo video of the Valentine's Day Update, which led to a 10% increase in orders and a 20% increase in total revenue!

My Role

As the only developer, I was responsbile for all aspects of the app outside of design.

Challenge

The project faced several key challenges:

  • Developing a mobile app quickly while maintaining high quality and performance
  • Managing 6,200 existing user accounts with duplicates, especailly when many users were not aware they had an account
  • Migrating from Shopify to Stripe payment processor mid-development
  • Building a cheap, maintainable solution

Solution

  • Developed a React Native app with TypeScript for type safety and better maintainability
  • Created an intuitive onboarding flow with OTP phone verification and account linking
  • Successfully migrated payment processing to Stripe, ensuring minimal disruption to users, and incorporating Apple Pay, Link, etc. for easier checkout
  • Leveraged as much functionality from Shopify as possible

Results

Since launching the app:

  • Over 6,000 active users at UW
  • Over 6,000 orders per month
  • Over 80% of orders are now through the mobile app
  • REV acquired Duffl, a formerly Y-Combinator backed startup, bringing scooter delivery to more campuses such as USC and UCLA
