MESH Fantasy Sports

React Native Figma
Website

Overview

MESH Fantasy Sports is a revolutionary fantasy sports platform that introduces squad-based management, allowing groups of friends or colleagues to collaboratively manage a fantasy team. The app raised thousands in funding after significant product management improvements that I helped implement.

My Role

As a Product Management Intern on this project, I was responsible for:

  • Conducting user research to identify key pain points and opportunities
  • Learning Figma to develop prototypes and mockups of new features
  • Writing functional requirement documents (FRDs) for new features and sprint planning documents
  • Remotely collaborating with the engineering team to implement new features

Learnings

This project reinforced several valuable product management principles:

First, I learned the importance of being able to dynamically change your product. In my 6 months at MESH, we underwent a full rebrand (formerly known as "Huddle Fantasy Sports") and changed many ideas from the original app.

By doing this, we were able to develop a better product that is raising backing from sponsors and puts on a path to spring us from 5 leagues to 2k+ users and beyond

I also gained a lot of technical experience working in flows that are industry standards (Jira, Figma, Standup Meetings, etc.) and learning from other devs with a lot more experience.
